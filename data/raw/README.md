# Raw Data

## Data Source

International student data can be obtained from the Australian Department of Education:
https://www.education.gov.au/

Housing vacancy rate data can be obtained from ABS or property market reports.

Accessed: March 31 2026

## Why Raw Data is Not Included

The raw data is not included in this repository because it is sourced from external providers and may be subject to access restrictions or updates over time. 

Including static copies of the data could lead to inconsistencies with the most recent available versions. Instead, this project documents the original data sources to ensure that the data can be independently obtained and reproduced by others.

## Variables

- suburb: Geographic location in Melbourne
- year: Year of observation
- international_students: Number of international students
- vacancy_rate: Residential vacancy rate (%)

## Limitations

The data may come from different sources and may not be perfectly aligned across time or geographic units. 

International student data and vacancy rate data may be measured differently, and there may be inconsistencies in reporting across sources.

Additionally, the data is not included in this repository and must be obtained externally.
